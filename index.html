COVID-19

A message from alffie's CEO

Paul Licuria

alffie CEO

As coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to evolve, we want to assure you that we are committed to providing a safe and healthy environment for our employees and maintaining continuity of our service to you and your clients. While our students may be in self-isolation, they will not be on their own. We have prepared and have initiated our emergency response procedures. Our Assessment and Support teams have transitioned to working remotely so we can continue to support our students throughout the upcoming shutdown period. Students will still be able to:

  • Access their online accounts and course material
  • Complete their coursework and online assessments
  • Receive coaching, mentoring and technical support via phone, email and live chat.

Our technology has been designed to support you and your clients remotely. With our in-house team of developers and technical support staff, we have the capability to continue to provide you and your clients with high-quality service during this challenging and uncertain time. Our platform can assist you to ensure the productivity of your team is maintained if they need to work from home. Our technology provides your employment consultants with detailed reports on student engagement and progression. So, with alffie, you can track and monitor your clients' progress remotely and ensure they achieve the required outcomes. You can also ensure that they continue to build their capacity and readiness for employment.

We are confident that coronavirus (COVID-19) will not significantly impact our ability to deliver our products and services to you. Again, we would like to reassure you that we will be here to support you during this period of unrest.

If you have any questions regarding our operations during this time, please contact us by phone on 1300 253 343 or by email at support@alffie.com.
