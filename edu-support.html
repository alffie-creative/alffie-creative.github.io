CHC30213 – Certificate III

Education Support

alffie’s CHC30213 – Certificate III in Education Support prepares students to assist with the education of children and young people, and provide them with encouragement and support in a classroom environment.

Throughout this course, students will develop the skills and knowledge to provide individual student support, help children and young people to improve their literacy and numeracy skills and support individuals with disability in the classroom.



Delivery mode

This course is delivered online through written and audiovisual lesson material and interactive assessment activities. It also requires practical work placement with an education provider, giving the student the real-world skills needed to work in education support. Prior to work placement, the student will need to obtain a National Police Check and Working With Children Check.



Course duration

Total: 1515 hours

Semester 1: 875 hours

Semester 2: 520 hours

Work placement: 120 hours

To achieve this qualification, the student must complete 120 hours of work placement as detailed in the Assessment Requirements of the units of competency. Please note, alffie will assist with the work placement process but does not guarantee practical placement.

Important note: Enrolment will be valid for up to 12 months.

*Participation in work placement is a compulsory requirement of these units.



It is a government requirement that students undertaking Nationally Recognised Training in Australia have a unique student identifier (USI). To begin this course, students will need a USI.

For more information on USIs, go to: www.usi.gov.au

In addition to a USI, to begin and complete this course, students will need:

  • Regular access to a computer, tablet or smartphone (note: there may be activities in the course that need to be completed using a desktop computer rather than a tablet or smartphone)
  • An email address and regular access to a reliable internet connection
  • Basic to intermediate computer, or digital device, navigation skills
  • To meet certain language, literacy and numeracy (LLN) requirements and pass a short LLN test
  • The ability and willingness to study online lesson material and complete online assessment activities, and to complete a practical work placement
  • PDF reader software (e.g., Adobe Acrobat) installed on their computer or the digital device they are using to access their course
  • The ability to communicate directly with alffie in English
  • The ability to understand and follow detailed instructions given verbally or written in English.



Outcome

All units in this course require work placement. To be issued with a CHC30213 – Certificate III in Education Support qualification, students will need to meet the online assessment and work placement requirements for all units in the course. Examples of job roles this qualification may be relevant to include:

  • Teachers’ aide
  • Education support worker
  • Tutor
  • Language and literacy worker
  • Learning support officer.
