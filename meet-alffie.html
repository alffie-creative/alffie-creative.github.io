Employment services

alffie has a range of training solutions available to support Job Active, Disability Employment Services (DES) and Transition To Work Program (TTW) providers help individuals transition into sustainable employment.

The theory components of our courses are delivered online and our custom built learning management system (LMS) has been designed with providers in mind. Enrolment, participation monitoring and reporting are all quick and easy.

Contact us to find out more.

Rehabilitation

alffie is passionate about helping people find long term, sustainable employment. We have a variety of courses available to support Occupational Rehabilitation providers help their New Employer Services (NES) clients achieve their employment goals.

Our courses range from those designed to develop employability skills or address non-vocational barriers and micro barriers to employment to those that provide Certificate II and III level qualifications. We understand the complexity of rehabilitation and are able to tailor our courses to meet individual NES clients’ needs.

Contact us to find out more.

Our obsessions

Create something to rave about

It’s simple really. We love what we do, and we aim to do it so well that our students and clients will feel inspired to tell anyone who will listen that they love what we do too.

First person on the sun

At alffie we believe that nothing is impossible. By being innovative, creative and really stretching the boundaries of online learning inspires us to change people's lives through education.

Make Mumma proud

As kids, before we do something we often ask ourselves 'would it make my mum proud?'. At alffie, we haven't grown out of asking ourselves this question. Or of letting the answer to it guide our actions. We know that no less than acting with integrity, being kind and demonstrating respect will make our mums (or our other moral measuring sticks) proud, so it's the choice we make—always, without exception.

One chorus, many voices

We are a group of individuals brought together by our shared passion for and commitment to creating inspiring online training. We know that it is when our individual efforts are driven by our shared passion that we achieve big things.

Make the dust, don't eat it

We are not content with simply meeting industry standards, we are obsessed with redefining and raising them. Our students and clients inspire us to be courageous, creative and to continually strive to become better and better..

Give Clark Kent the cape

We truly believe everyone can achieve their dreams. Whether members of our own team, students or clients, to us ‘giving Clark Kent the cape’ means doing whatever we can to empower them to do so.
